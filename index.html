Z 900

The powerful engine and chassis, designed to work in perfect synergy, result in the Z900's exceptional performance and all-around capabilities, suitable for riders of all skill levels. Its responsive and friendly character (enhanced with traction control for added safety) is complemented by agile handling and a responsive, precise throttle, as always. The Z900 is ideally suited for city riding with ease, but because it's designed to deliver maximum exhilarating performance, its most natural and effective riding environment is on winding roads and open streets.

  • Maximum speed: 300 km/h
  • Horsepower: 500 HP
  • Engine: V8
